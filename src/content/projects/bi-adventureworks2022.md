## Overview

A complete business intelligence stack implementation that processes the AdventureWorks2022 dataset through ETL pipelines, multidimensional modeling, and interactive dashboards.

## Problem

Organizations need to transform raw transactional data into actionable business insights. The challenge is building reliable ETL pipelines, designing performant analytical models, and creating intuitive visualizations that decision-makers can use.

## Solution

This solution implements the full Microsoft BI stack: SSIS packages for ETL data transformation, SSAS multidimensional cubes for analytical querying, and Power BI dashboards for interactive reporting across sales, inventory, and customer analytics.

## Main Features

- **ETL pipelines**: SSIS packages for data extraction, cleansing, and loading
- **Multidimensional cubes**: SSAS models with pre-aggregated measures for fast queries
- **Interactive dashboards**: Power BI reports with drill-down and filtering
- **KPI tracking**: Sales performance, inventory turnover, and customer segmentation metrics

## My Contribution

Designed and implemented the complete BI solution. Built SSIS ETL flows for data integration, developed SSAS cubes with calculated measures and KPIs, and created Power BI dashboards with DAX-based analytics.

## Challenges

Optimizing cube processing performance for large volumes, designing dimension hierarchies that support complex analytical queries, and ensuring data consistency across the ETL pipeline.
