## Overview

An enterprise resource planning solution tailored for supermarket operations, covering inventory management, point-of-sale integration, supplier relationships, and business intelligence reporting.

## Problem

Supermarket chains need an integrated system to manage complex inventory flows, track sales across multiple locations, coordinate with suppliers, and generate real-time operational reports. Disconnected tools lead to stock discrepancies and delayed decision-making.

## Solution

Built a centralized ERP system with a Spring Boot backend, MySQL database, and Angular frontend. The system synchronizes inventory across locations, processes sales transactions, manages purchase orders, and provides role-based dashboards for different operational roles.

## Main Features

- **Inventory management**: Real-time stock tracking with automatic reorder alerts
- **Point-of-sale integration**: Transaction processing with barcode scanning support
- **Supplier management**: Purchase order workflow and supplier performance tracking
- **Reporting dashboards**: Sales analytics, stock turnover, and profit margin reports
- **Multi-location support**: Centralized database with location-specific views
